## Our Purpose

Play Harmonium is a browser-based harmonium practice tool for singers, students, teachers, and devotional music learners who need a simple way to practice without installing software.

The project focuses on making harmonium practice easier to start. You can open the keyboard in a browser, choose note labels, use touch or computer keys, connect a MIDI keyboard, and practice Sargam patterns, bhajan phrases, vocal warm-ups, or basic melody work.

## Why We Built It

Many learners search for a web harmonium because they need quick pitch support while practicing at home, in a class, or during rehearsal. A physical harmonium is still the best long-term instrument for serious study, but a lightweight online tool can help when you only need to find Sa, rehearse a phrase, or check a melody.

Play Harmonium was built to serve that practical moment: fast loading, clear note labels, responsive controls, and learning guides that explain how to use the tool in real practice.

## What You Can Do Here

- Play a free harmonium keyboard directly in your browser.
- Switch between western note labels and Sargam-style practice.
- Use transpose and octave controls to find a comfortable vocal range.
- Connect a MIDI keyboard for more natural practice.
- Read beginner guides about harmonium notes, Sargam, bhajan practice, and vocal warm-ups.

## Editorial Approach

Our guides are written for practical learners. We aim to keep explanations clear, examples usable, and recommendations grounded in real practice situations rather than abstract music theory alone.

When a topic needs deeper musical judgment, we keep the advice modest and encourage learners to follow their teacher, gharana, choir director, or local tradition.
